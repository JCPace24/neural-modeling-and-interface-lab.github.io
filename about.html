On Memory Neurotechnologies

Our Mission

To develop brain-like devices that can mimic and restore cognitive functions.

Our Methods

  • Ultraminiaturized wireless bioelectronics systems for chronic neural recording and stimulation.
  • Electrophysiological recordings and stimulation in hippocampal slices, behaving animals, and epilepsy human patients.
  • Input-output nonlinear dynamical modeling of the hippocampus.
  • Full-scale biologically realistic modeling of the hippocampus.
