Computational Modeling

We combine mechanistic and input-output modeling approaches to build computational models for investigating the underlying mechanisms of memory functions and developing cortical prostheses for restoring/enhancing memory functions.  

Neural Interface Development

We develop minaturized devices for chronic, multi-region, large-scale recording and stimulation of the nervous system.
