Computational Modeling

We synergistically combine mechanistic and input-output modeling approaches to build computational models to (1) investigate the underlying mechanisms of learning and memory, and (2) develop hippocampal prostheses to restore and enhance memory functions lost in diseases or injuries.

Neural Interface Development

We design and test miniaturized devices for chronic, wireless, multi-region, large-scale recording and stimulation of the nervous system in untethered animals to (1) study neural functions during naturalistic behaviors, and (2) develop implantable biomimetic neural prostheses and neuromodulation systems.
